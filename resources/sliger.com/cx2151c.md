## Overview

15" 2U | 2x External 3.5" Bays | MicroATX | 2-Slot GPU. 2x external 3.5" adapter bays and 2x internal 2.5" SSD mounts. Mounts up to 3x 80mm fans; supports air coolers up to 66mm tall. Supports Micro-ATX and Mini-ITX motherboards. SFX or SFX-L PSU compatibility. Full Height Dual-Slot GPU via PCIe Gen 5 x16 riser. Made in USA with US materials.
