## Overview

13" 2U | 2x External 3.5" Bays | MicroATX | SFX PSU. 2x external 3.5" adapter bays and 2x internal 2.5" SSD mounts. Mounts up to 3x 80mm fans; supports air coolers up to 66mm tall. Supports MicroATX and Deep MicroATX motherboards. SFX or SFX-L PSU compatibility. 5x PCIe slots for Half-Height Full-Length PCIe cards. Made in USA with US materials.
