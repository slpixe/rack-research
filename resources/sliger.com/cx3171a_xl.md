- **Product Page:** [CX3171a XL](https://sliger.com/products/cx3171a_xl)

- **Price:** $329.00 (Base Price)

## Dimensions

- Mini-DTX
- Deep Mini-ITX
- FlexATX
- MicroATX
- Deep MicroATX
- ATX
- EATX
- SSI-CEB
- SSI-EEB
- XL-ATX/EATX (up to 12.8" x 13")

## Power Supply Support
- SFX
- SFX-L

## Cooling Support
- **CPU Air Cooler:** Max Height 110mm
- **Liquid Cooling:**
  - 1x 120mm, 2x 120mm, 1x 240mm, or 1x 360mm radiator
  - Max radiator length: 395mm (360mm)
- **Fans:**
  - Front: 3x 120mm (up to 38mm thick)
  - Rear: 1x 120mm fan (optional PCIe slot to fan adapters)

## PCIe / GPU Support
- **Slots:** 3x Full Height Horizontal PCIe slots (via PCIe 5.0 16x riser)
- **Max GPU Width:** 72mm (3.5-slot)
- **Max GPU Height:** 150mm
- **Max GPU Length:**
  - 375mm (with 25mm front fans)
  - 346mm (with 360mm AIO + 25mm fans)

## Storage
- 8x 2.5" drives or 3x 3.5" drives (via internal bracket)

## Features
- PCIe 5.0 16x riser for horizontal GPU mounting
- Rear support bridge to prevent GPU sag
- Washable/reusable dust filter included
- Planned availability through 2032
- Made in USA
