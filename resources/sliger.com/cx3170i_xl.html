    GPU / PCIe Cards
    Eight (8) Full height PCIe card slot cutouts
    • 8x full height PCIe card slots
    • Maximum card height: 115mm (including power connectors)
    • Maximum card length with 2x 25mm thick front fans: 375mm
    • Maximum card length with Asetek 240mm AIO and 2x 25mm thick front fans: 347mm
    • Maximum card length with Asetek 240mm AIO and 4x 25mm thick front fans: 322mm

      • Total Internal Depth: 395mm
      • Thickness of radiator + thickness of case fans + GPU length must equal less than 395mm total.
    Storage
    One (1) 2.5" drive
    • Supports up to 1x 2.5" SSD under 5.25" bay
    One (1) 2.5" drive up to 15mm thick
    • Supports up to 1x 2.5" SSD up to 7mm thick, or 1x 2.5" SSD up to 15mm thick
    • Mounting points located between motherboard IO shield and SFX PSU cutout

    • Sliger recommends use of M.2/NVMe and PCIe based storage on motherboard when possible
    • Motherboard PCIe slots can be utilized for M.2 drives via Sabrent PCIe to M.2 or equivalent
    5.25" Bays

    • 5.25" bay filler blank provides mounting for two(2) 2.5" SSD and one(1) 3.5" HDD
