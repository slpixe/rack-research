## Overview

The CX4150i is a 15" deep 4U rackmount chassis featuring one external 5.25" bay. It supports EATX and SSI-CEB motherboards and maintains support for up to a 360mm AIO liquid cooler.

### PSU Support

- ATX (Maximum length 268mm including connectors)
- CRPS (Redundant Power - ATX form factor)

### Storage

- 1x External 5.25" Bay (removable filler blank)
- 2x 2.5" SSD (up to 7mm thick) OR 1x 2.5" SSD (up to 15mm thick) - Rear mounting
- 5.25" filler blank provides mounting for 2x 2.5" SSD and 1x 3.5" HDD

### Cooling

- Front: 3x 120mm fans (up to 38mm thick)
- Rear: Optional bracket for 1x 120mm fan over IO shield
- Rear: Optional bracket for 1x 120mm fan over PCIe cards

### Front Panel I/O

- 1x Momentary Switch / Power Status LED (White)
- 2x USB 3.0 Type A
- 1x USB 3.2 Type C (10Gbps)
