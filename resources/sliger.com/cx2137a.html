CX2137a13" 2U | 4x 80mm Fan Mounts | EATX/ATX | FlexATX PSU

  • Internal storage for up to 5x 2.5" SSD or 2x 3.5" HDD

  • FlexATX PSU compatibility up to 150mm long
  • 7x PCIe slots for Half-Height Full-Length PCIe cards
  • Made in USA with US materials

Technical Specifications

Motherboard
Mini-ITX (L x W: 170mm x 170mm) [6.7" x 6.7"]
Mini-DTX (L x W: 203mm x 170mm) [8" x 6.7"]
FlexATX (L x W: 229mm x 191mm) [9" x 7.5"]
MicroATX (L x W: 245mm x 245mm) [9.6" x 9.6"]
ATX (L x W: 305mm x 244mm) [12" x 9.6"]
SSI-CEB (L x W: 305mm x 267mm) [12" x 10.5"]
SSI-CEB motherboards are incompatible with 38mm thick fans
EATX (L x W: 305mm x 267mm) [12" x 10.5"]
EATX motherboards over 10.25" deep are incompatible with 38mm thick fans
CPU Air Coolers
Max Height: 66mm
  • RECOMMENDED CPU COOLERS
  • Sliger recommends theID Cooling IS-55 CPU Coolerfor most builds.
  • Performance and reliability of the IS-55 can be enhanced by swapping the stock fan for a A12x15 120mm Slim Fan.
  • Server grade and HEDT 2U CPU coolers are available for all CPUs and sockets through Dynatron or CoolJag USA.
  • For more options, see this PCPartPicker.com list of compatible retail CPU coolers.
  • CAUTION: 60mm or taller "Top Down Coolers" such as Noctua L65 are NOT recommended.
    Filter Foam
    Includes one (1) washable / reusable dust filter
    • 30 ppi open cell reticulated polyether filter foam with flame resistant rating, UL94 HF1, UL FILE NO E93229(N)
    • Alternate density and/or materials such as aluminum mesh available for OEM projects
    Fan Mounting
    Front
    Mounting for four (4) 80mm fans
    • Mounts up to 4x 80mm fans up to 38mm thick
    • NOTE: SSI-CEB and 10.5" deep EATX motherboards are incompatible with 38mm thick fans

    • RECOMMENDED FANS
    • Noctua NF-A8 PWM
    • Thermalright TL-B8
    • Arctic P8 MAX
    • Server and HEDT CPUs may require very high airflow fans such as ARCTIC S8038-10K or equivalent.
      GPU / PCIe Cards
      Seven (7) Half height PCIe card slot cutouts
      • 7x Half Height PCIe card slots
      • Maximum card height: 72mm - measured from bottom edge of PCIe finger tab to lid of case
      • Max Length with 25mm fans: 276mm - including power connectors
      • Max Length with 38mm fan: 263mm - including power connectors
        • Total Internal Depth: 302mm
        • Thickness of case fans + GPU/PCIe card length must equal less than 302mm total.

        • Half height vented slot covers are provided with case
        • Note: Cards may be referred to as: 2U height, low-profile, or MD1/MD2/MD3 form factor

        • Compatible GPUs
        • Gigabyte 4060 TI 8GB Low Profile
        • Intel ARC A310 single slot
        • A380 dual slot
        • AMD RX 550 LP
        • Nvidia A4000 SFF
        • Nvidia A2000
      Storage
      Five (5) 2.5" drives up to 7mm thick
      • Up to five(5) 2.5" SSDs when not using 3.5" mounts
      Two (2) 3.5" drives up to 20mm thick
      • Up to two(2) 3.5" HDDs
      • OR
      • One(1) full-sized 3.5" HDD, and one(1) 2.5" SSD up to 15mm thick

      • Sliger recommends use of M.2/NVMe and PCIe based storage on motherboard when possible
      • Motherboard PCIe slots can be utilized for M.2 drives via Sabrent PCIe to M.2 or equivalent
      Power Supply
      One (1) FlexATX power supply
      • Maximum Length: 210mm
      • Note: FlexATX PSUs over 150mm long will interfere with hardware for 'c' and 'x' variants

      • Recommended Models:
      • SilverStone SST-FX600-PT 600W
      • FSP FSP500-57FDB 500W
      • FSP FSP300-57FCB 300W
      Rack Rails
      General Devices C300-S Series

      Rack Rails are not included by default, must be purchased on the configuration or parts page.

      Other Brand Compatibility
      • Brands such as iStarUSA TC Rails are natively compatible.
