## Overview

13" 2U | 4x 80mm Fan Mounts | MicroATX | SFX PSU. Mounting for up to 4x 80mm fans; supports air coolers up to 66mm tall. Supports MicroATX and Deep MicroATX motherboards. Mounting for SFX or SFX-L form factor power supplies. 5x PCIe slots for Half Height Full-Length PCIe cards. Made in USA with US materials.
