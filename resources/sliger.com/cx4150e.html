Sliger CX4150e

Source: https://sliger.com/products/cx4150e

Overview

The CX4150e is a 15" deep 4U rackmount chassis featuring two external 5.25" bays. It supports EATX and SSI-CEB motherboards and supports up to a 240mm AIO liquid cooler.
