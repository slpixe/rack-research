Sliger CX4150a

Source: https://sliger.com/products/cx4150a

Overview

The CX4150a is a 15" deep 4U rackmount chassis designed for high-performance workstations and servers. It supports EATX and SSI-CEB motherboards and features extensive liquid cooling support with up to a 360mm AIO.
