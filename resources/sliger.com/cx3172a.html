      GPU / PCIe Cards
      Five (5) Full height PCIe card slot cutouts
      • 5x full height PCIe card slots
      • Maximum card height: 115mm (including power connectors)
      • Maximum card length with 3x 25mm thick front fans: 375mm
      • Maximum card length with Asetek 360mm AIO and 3x 25mm thick front fans: 347mm
      • Maximum card length with Asetek 360mm AIO and 6x 25mm thick front fans: 322mm

      • Total Internal Depth: 395mm
      • Thickness of radiator + thickness of case fans + GPU length must equal less than 395mm total.
