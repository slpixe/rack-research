### Depth Categories
- **Short depth**: 25cm - 40cm (suitable for wall-mount racks or compact installations)
- **Medium depth**: 45cm - 55cm (standard rack depth)
- **Long depth**: 61cm - 66cm (deep server racks, more drive bays)

### Form Factor Support
- **mini ITX**: Smallest boards, typically 1U cases
- **micro ATX**: Small-medium boards, 1U-2U cases
- **ATX**: Standard boards, 2U-4U cases
- **E-ATX**: Extended boards, larger 3U-4U cases

### Manufacturers Featured
- **IPC**: Primary brand (most products)
- **Chenbro**: Enterprise-grade cases
- **Supermicro**: Server-grade with integrated PSU
- **Silverstone**: Consumer/prosumer grade

### Special Purpose Cases
- Mining chassis (GPU-focused)
- Storage cases (multiple drive bays)
- Front-access designs
- Cases with integrated backplanes
