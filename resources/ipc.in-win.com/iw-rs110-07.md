## Overview

InWin IW-RS110-07 is a 1U high-density NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 10 hot-swappable 2.5" U.2 NVMe/SAS/SATA SSD bays. Features SlimSAS and OCuLink backplane options for maximum storage density.
