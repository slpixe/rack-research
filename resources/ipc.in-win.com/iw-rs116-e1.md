## Overview

InWin IW-RS116-E1 is a high-density 1U PCIe Gen5 rackmount chassis specifically designed for E1.S NVMe storage, supporting up to 16 hot-swappable E1.S NVMe SSDs. Features two PCIe Gen5 backplanes with MCIO 8i connectors and optimized cooling for next-generation storage demands.

|------|----------|----------|
| E1.S NVMe (15mm) | 16 | Yes (tool-less) |

### Storage Configuration

- **Organization**: Two modular cages with 8 bays each
- **Backplanes**: Two pre-installed PCIe Gen5 backplanes
- **Connectors**: MCIO 8i connectors
- **Speed**: PCIe Gen5 - 32 GT/s per lane (double PCIe Gen4 bandwidth)
- **Tool-less Design**: Hot-swappable and tool-less drive trays

### Storage Notes

- Expandable via optional retimer cards (IW-RT04-02) when motherboard lacks sufficient slots
- Retimer cards not included
