## Overview

InWin IW-RS208-07 is a 2U NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 8 hot-swappable 2.5"/3.5" drive bays with flexible NVMe/SAS/SATA configuration. Designed for high-performance and HPC environments requiring hybrid storage flexibility.

### Backplane Features

**Hybrid Backplane Options:**
- **Mini-SAS HD (SFF-8643)** connectors for SAS/SATA
- **OCuLink (SFF-8611)** connectors for NVMe
- **Passive backplane** design
- **PCIe Gen4** support (up to 16GT/s)
- **SAS3** support (up to 12Gbps)

### Storage Notes

- Tool-free tray design for easy drive maintenance
- Supports both fast storage (NVMe) and high capacity (SAS/SATA) simultaneously
