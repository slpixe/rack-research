Product Description

InWin IW-RF100S is a compact 1U short-depth rackmount chassis specifically designed for Mini-ITX motherboards. At only 250mm depth and 5.31 litres, it's ideal for shallow rack installations.

Key Feature: Reversible design allows motherboard I/O ports to be presented at the front or rear. Rack ears fit both ends of the chassis for flexible installation.

Tool-free quick swap drive cages and support for up to 4x 2.5" drives make it perfect for compact server and storage applications.
