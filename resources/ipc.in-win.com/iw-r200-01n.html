Product Description

InWin IW-R200-01N is a 2U open-bay long-depth rackmount server chassis supporting ATX, CEB, and EEB motherboards. Extended depth at 661mm accommodates larger motherboards and components.

Key Features: Long depth design for EEB motherboard support, 2x 5.25" external and 6x 3.5" internal drive bays, 7 low-profile PCIe slots or optional 3 full-height, 28" tool-less & ball-bearing slide rails.

Ideal for enterprise servers, storage servers, and workstations requiring maximum motherboard compatibility and internal space.
