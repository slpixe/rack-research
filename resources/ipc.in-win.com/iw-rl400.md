## Overview

InWin IW-RL400 is a 4U liquid-cooled rackmount chassis designed for AI applications, including LLM and generative AI workloads. Pre-installed with an industrial-grade 360mm AIO liquid cooler, it ensures optimal cooling for high system loads. With support for eight full-height, full-length PCIe slots and flexible configuration options, it is ideal for AI GPU edge servers.

## Key Features

- Liquid-cooled 4U rackmount server
- Pre-installed 360mm AIO liquid cooler with three 120mm fans
- AIO options available with Intel® LGA4710 bracket, or with LGA1851 and AMD AM5 mounting package
- Supports up to five 3.5" or four 2.5" scalable storage drives
- Optional rear fans: Two 80x25mm and one additional 80x38mm fan kit for enhanced GPU cooling
- Supports up to 8 FHFL PCIe slots for multi-task AI machine training
- Compatible with ATX up to 2500W and CRPS 80 PLUS® Platinum power supplies
