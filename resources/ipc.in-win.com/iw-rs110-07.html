Product Description

InWin IW-RS110-07 is a 1U high-density NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 10 hot-swappable 2.5" U.2 NVMe/SAS/SATA SSD bays with screwless trays. Features flexible backplane options with SlimSAS and OCuLink connectors.

Key Features: Maximum storage density with 10 hot-swappable bays, flexible backplane connectivity (Mini-SAS HD, SlimSAS, or OCuLink), tool-less design for quick maintenance, low acoustic noise, and optional redundant power supply.

Designed for workstation, data center, cloud, virtualization, high-density storage, and supercomputer applications requiring maximum 2.5" storage in minimal rack space.
