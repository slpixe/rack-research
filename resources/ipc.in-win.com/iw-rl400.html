IW-RL400

4U Liquid-Cooled Rackmount Chassis

Features

  • Liquid-cooled 4U rackmount server
  • Pre-installed 360 mm AIO liquid cooler with three 120 mm fans
  • AIO options are available with an Intel® LGA4710 bracket, or with LGA1851 and AMD AM5 mounting package
  • Supports up to five 3.5" or four 2.5" scalable storage drives
  • Optional rear fans: Two 80 x 25 mm and one additional 80 x 38 mm fan kit for enhanced GPU cooling
  • Supports up to 8 FHFL PCIe slots for multi-task AI machine training
  • Compatible with ATX up to 2500W and CRPS 80 PLUS® Platinum power supplies
