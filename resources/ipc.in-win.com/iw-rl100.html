Product Description

InWin IW-RL100 is a 1U liquid-cooled rackmount chassis with pre-installed 120mm AIO liquid CPU cooler. Designed for edge server applications, industrial automation, and AI servers where enhanced cooling is critical in compact environments.

Key Features: Pre-installed liquid cooling system with 4200 RPM pump, three high-performance 40mm fans at 14,500 RPM, and support for both Intel and AMD platforms across multiple generations.

The dual cooling system (liquid for CPU + high-speed fans) ensures stable, energy-efficient operation for processors with high thermal demands.
