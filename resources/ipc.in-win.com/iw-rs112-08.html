Product Description

InWin IW-RS112-08 is a 1U PCIe Gen5 NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 12 hot-swappable 2.5" U.2/U.3 NVMe SSDs with MCIO 4i connectors for next-generation PCIe Gen5 speeds at 32GT/s per lane.

Key Features: Hybrid backplane with MCIO 4i (NVMe Gen5) and SlimSAS (SAS/SATA) connectors, UBM management for NVMe status, high power delivery for fully loaded configurations, and backward compatibility with previous generation storage.

Each bay supports either SAS/SATA or NVMe (not simultaneously). Requires MCIO-compatible motherboard, HBA/RAID, or PCIe retimer card. Ideal for cloud storage, virtualization, high-performance databases, and AI/ML workloads.
