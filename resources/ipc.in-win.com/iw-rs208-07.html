Product Description

InWin IW-RS208-07 is a 2U NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 8 hot-swappable drive bays with flexible NVMe/SAS/SATA configuration. Choose between 8 SAS/SATA drives or 4 SAS/SATA + 4 U.2 NVMe drives.

Key Features: Hybrid backplane with Mini-SAS HD and OCuLink connectors, PCIe Gen4 support, 2x rear hot-swap system disk bays, 4 hot-swap fans, redundant CRPS PSU, and tool-free drive trays.

Designed for high-performance storage, HPC, virtualization, databases, and hybrid cloud applications requiring both speed (NVMe) and capacity (SAS/SATA) in a 2U form factor.
