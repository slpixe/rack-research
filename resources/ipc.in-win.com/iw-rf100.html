Product Description

InWin IW-RF100 is a 1U short-depth rackmount chassis supporting Mini-ITX and ATX motherboards. At 365.9mm depth, it's designed for compact rack installations.

Key Feature: Reversible design allows motherboard I/O ports to be presented at the front or rear. Rack ears fit both ends of the chassis for flexible installation.

Tool-free 3.5" & 2.5" HDD cage design with anti-vibration fans make it ideal for versatile server applications.
