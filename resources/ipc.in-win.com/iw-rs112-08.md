## Overview

InWin IW-RS112-08 is a 1U PCIe Gen5 NVMe hybrid rackmount storage server supporting up to 12 hot-swappable 2.5" U.2/U.3 NVMe SSDs. Features hybrid backplane with MCIO 4i connectors for PCIe Gen5 speeds (32GT/s) and SlimSAS for SAS/SATA compatibility.

### Backplane Features

**Hybrid Backplane - PCIe Gen5 & SAS4:**
- **MCIO 4i Connectors**: 3x MCIO 4i for NVMe (12 drives total via PCIe Gen5)
- **SlimSAS Connectors**: 3x SFF-8654 SlimSAS 4i for SAS/SATA
- **Speed**: PCIe Gen5 (32GT/s per lane), SAS4 (24Gbps)
- **Backward Compatible**: SAS3, SAS 6Gbps, SATA 6Gbps, SATA 3Gbps
- **Management**: UBM (Universal Backplane Management) for NVMe LED/status, SGPIO for SAS/SATA
- **Power Delivery**: Up to 32A at 12V, 24A at 5V

### Storage Notes

- Each bay supports either SAS/SATA OR NVMe (not simultaneously)
- Requires MCIO-compatible motherboard, HBA/RAID, or PCIe retimer card for Gen5 speeds

## Technical Notes

MCIO (Mini Cool Edge IO) provides:
- Up to 4 PCIe lanes per connector
- 64 GT/s total bandwidth per MCIO 4i connector
- Sideband signals for disk activity, fail, and locate indicators
- Requires compatible Gen5 HBA or motherboard
