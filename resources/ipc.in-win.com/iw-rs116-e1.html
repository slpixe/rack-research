Product Description

InWin IW-RS116-E1 is a high-density 1U PCIe Gen5 rackmount chassis specifically designed for E1.S NVMe storage. Supports up to 16 hot-swappable E1.S NVMe SSDs organized in two modular cages with 8 bays each.

Key Features: Two pre-installed PCIe Gen5 backplanes with MCIO 8i connectors, six high-speed fans at up to 33,000 RPM for optimal thermal management, tool-less hot-swappable drive trays, and dual redundant 750W PSU.

Engineered for data centers, cloud service providers, and enterprises needing high IOPS and dense, high-speed storage solutions. The E1.S form factor provides enhanced thermal management and highest density in 1U, optimized for PCIe Gen5 speeds.
