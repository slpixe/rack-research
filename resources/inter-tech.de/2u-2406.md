# IPC 2U-2406

The 2U 2406 is the perfect rack for a low-priced storage system.

The excellent quality of the materials combined with superb workmanship establishes the basis for a very variable case.

The 2U 2406 offers the possibility to mount maximal six 2.5" or 3.5" Hot-Swap HDDs, one 5.25" external drive and one 2.5" internal drive. In scope of delivery is a 5.25" drive bay to mount 4x 2.5" or 1x 3.5" HDDs, if you don ́t need a 5.25" drive. The 2U 2406 is equipped with four serial 80mm fans behind the drive bays to ensure a solid cooling.

Both serial USB 3.0 front connectors round off the whole package.
