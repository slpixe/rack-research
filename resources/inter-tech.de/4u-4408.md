# 4U-4408

The 4U 4408 is the perfect rack for a low-priced storage system. The excellent quality of the materials combined with superb workmanship establishes the basis for a very variable case.

The 4U 4408 offers the possibility to mount maximal eight 2.5" or 3.5" Hot-Swap HDDs and one internal 2.5" or 3.5" HDD (by using a 2.5" with 9.5mm height also a 3.5" HDD simultaneously). The 4U 4408 is equipped with two serial 80mm fans.
