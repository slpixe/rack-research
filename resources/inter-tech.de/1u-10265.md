# IPC 1U-10265

The 1U 10265 is the perfect rack for a low-priced server system. The excellent quality of the materials combined with superb workmanship establishes the basis for a very variable case.

The 1U 10265 provides the possibility to mount nine 2.5" and three 3.5" internal hard drives. Five standard 40mm fans guarantee an optimal cooling. Additional extras like easily reachable USB 3.0 connectors and the universal ATX I/O plate complete this total package.
