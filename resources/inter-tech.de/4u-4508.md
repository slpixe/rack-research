# 4U-4508

The IPC 4U-4508 is a perfect case for a cost-efficient Mini Server System. Using high-level material combined with excellent workmanship we created a variable and reliable industrial case.

So the 4U-4508 supports ATX or PS/2 redundant power supplies, up to eight 2.5"/3.5" Hot-Plug hard disks, two internal 2.5" and two external 5.25" devices and mainboards up to E-ATX size.

Furthermore the case has two 80mm fans, space for two additional 80mm fans and two USB 3.0 front ports.
