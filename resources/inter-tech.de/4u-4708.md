# 4U-4708

The IPC 4U-4708 is a service-friendly storage enclosure with 8 hot-swap removable frames.

Four 80 mm fans in the front of the case can be easily replaced by open the front flap. The fans are supplied with power centrally via a Molex connection. In addition, two 80 mm fans are installed in the rear panel, which together ensure a perfect air flow.

The built-in backplane has two SFF 8643 connectors for connection to a controller.
