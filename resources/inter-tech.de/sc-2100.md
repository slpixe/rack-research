# SC-2100

The SC-2100 is an enclosure for Mini-ITX motherboards with a dual 2.5"/ 3.5" Hot-Swap frame in the front, which is located behind a lockable front door.

Another external 2.5" Hot-Swap frame is located on the back of the enclosure cage.

The standard 60mm fan ensures clean air and thus a perfect cooling behavior inside. A slot for a Flex-ATX power supply as well as a USB 2.0 and a USB 3.0 front connector complete the package.
