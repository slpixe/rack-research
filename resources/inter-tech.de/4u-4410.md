# 4U-4410

The 4U 4410 is the perfect rack for a low-priced storage system. The excellent quality of the materials combined with superb workmanship establishes the basis for a very variable case.

The 4U 4410 offers the possibility to mount a maximum of ten 2.5" or 3.5" Hot-Swap HDDs, four internal 2.5" HDDs and three external 5.25" drives. The 4U 4410 is equipped with three serial 120mm Hot-Swap fans behind the drive bays. Two further 80mm fans in the rear of the case ensure optimal cooling.
