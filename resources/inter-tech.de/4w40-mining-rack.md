# IPC 4W40 MINING-RACK

The 4W40 is a 4U-rack is designed to mount up to 14 graphics cards and 2 power supply units.

Thanks to the superb workmanship it is the ideal case for your sensitive hardware.

Six pre-installed 120mm fans ensure an optimal airflow and cooling of your cooling of your components. Two USB 2.0 ports complete the offer.
