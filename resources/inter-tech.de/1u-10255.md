# IPC 1U-10255

The 1U 10255 is the perfect rack for a low-priced server system. The excellent quality of the materials combined with superb workmanship establishes the basis for a very variable case.

The 1U 10255 provides the possibility to mount nine 2.5" and one 3.5" internal hard drives. Five standard 40mm fans guarantee an optimal cooling. Additional extras like easily reachable USB 3.0 and USB 2.0 connectors and the universal ATX I/O plate complete this total package.
