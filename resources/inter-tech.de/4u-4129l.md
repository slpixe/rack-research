# IPC 4U-4129L

The IPC 4U 4129L is a 4U case with a depth of 695mm and thus offers sufficient space for high-performance server systems.

In the front are two removable drive cages mounted with integrated 120mm fans, of which each offers space for up to 5x 3.5" hard drives, which can be installed without screws. 3x 5.25" removable frames can be easily installed per drive cage, which can provide easier access to the the hard drives (optionally available).

Five additional fans in the center bar and on the back also ensure optimal cooling of the entire system.
