# IPC 4F28 MINING-RACK

The 4F28 Mining is a 19" rack case with 4U and an installation depth of 650mm.

In addition to accommodating a motherboard with a maximum of 12" x 13", it offers space for up to 28x 3.5" HDD and up to 4x 2.5" or 6x¹ 2.5" SSD. Thanks to its robust construction, the case offers the necessary support for your sensitive hardware.

Six powerful 120mm case fans are preinstalled for optimal cooling of the hardware, three of them in the front and three more in the middle bar. Two USB 3.0 front connections complete the offer.
