# IPC 4W2 MINING-RACK

The 4W2 is a 4U-rack with a depth of 635mm and is designed to mount up to 8 graphics cards.

Thanks to the superb workmanship its the ideal case for your sensitive hardware.

Six pre-installed 120mm fans ensure an optimal airflow and cooling of your components. To round it up, two USB 2.0 ports complete the offer.
