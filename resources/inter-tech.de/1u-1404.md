# IPC 1U-1404

The 1U 1404 is the ideal case for a small and cheap server solution. With its excellent quality and finish, Inter-Tech established the basis for a very variable case.

The 1U 1404 offers the possibility to install maximal four 2.5" or 3.5" Hot-Swap HDDs, one Slim DVD external and three internal 2.5" HDDs. The 1U 1404 has five 40mm fans included, which are mounted directly at the back of the drive brackets and ensure therefore a reliable and optimal cooling.
