## Overview

Professional 4U 19" rackmount chassis designed for data centers, labs, and SMEs. Features 360mm liquid cooling compatibility (mounting bracket included), high-strength steel construction, modern connectivity including USB Type-C, key lock security, and anti-intrusion system. Includes 2x 80mm rear fans.
