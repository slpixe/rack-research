# UK2130 2U600 UK51922

## Overview
The UNYKA PRO UK2130 2U600 is a versatile 2U rackmount server chassis engineered for advanced compatibility and flexible deployment. With a 600 mm depth, this chassis supports standard ATX motherboards and power supplies, and offers seven PCI expansion slots to maximize configuration options. Its front panel includes USB Type-C connectivity and features full ventilation to enhance airflow. It also comes with pre-installed mounting holes for sliding rails, making installation in standard racks quick and convenient.

## Internal Architecture and Power Systems
Its 600 mm depth provides generous internal space, allowing the installation of ATX and MATX motherboards and supporting a wide range of server configurations. The chassis is fully compatible with standard ATX power supplies without requiring additional brackets, ensuring a secure and professional installation.

## Internal Storage Capacity
The internal storage layout supports up to two 2.5" SSDs and six 3.5" hard drives, providing the flexibility to build mixed storage configurations that combine SSD performance with traditional HDD capacity. This setup is ideal for implementing tiered storage solutions or RAID systems tailored to the specific requirements of each project.

## Cooling System and Thermal Management
The chassis features an optimized thermal design with a perforated front panel that ensures unrestricted airflow. The top cover is also micro-perforated above the ATX power supply to enhance ventilation and overall heat dissipation. The cooling system includes two 80 mm front fans and three 80 mm middle fans with native PWM control for efficient temperature regulation. This active airflow configuration delivers optimal thermal performance even under heavy workloads.

## High-Performance Hardware Compatibility
Engineered to support high-performance components, the chassis provides ample space for next-generation graphics cards, including top-tier models such as the NVIDIA RTX 4090. This makes it an ideal solution for compute-intensive applications, professional rendering workflows, and high-performance workstation environments.

## Rack Installation and Rail Compatibility
The chassis is designed for fast and straightforward installation in standard server racks, featuring pre-installed mounting holes compatible with 22" sliding rack rails. This setup streamlines deployment in data center environments and simplifies maintenance operations by allowing full system extraction. For optimal integration, the use of UNYKA PRO’s UK52094 rail kit is recommended, available as an optional accessory.

## Front I/O Ports
The front panel includes:
- On/Off Button: Safe system power control for convenient operation.
- USB 2.0 Port: Ideal for peripherals and general-purpose devices.
- USB 3.0 Port: Fast and stable transfers for high-performance accessories and external drives.
- Reset Button: System reboot functionality.
- True USB Type-C 3.2 Port (up to 10 Gbps): Maximum speed and full compatibility with modern devices—perfect for professionals who require fast and efficient data throughput.
