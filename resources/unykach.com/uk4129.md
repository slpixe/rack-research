## Overview

4U 19" rackmount chassis with extensive motherboard compatibility including E-ATX, ATX, MicroATX, Mini-ITX, and SSI-EEB. Features key lock security, anti-dust filter, 3 external 5.25" bays, 8 internal 3.5" bays for high storage expandability. Matte black finish with strong front handles for easy rack insertion/removal.
