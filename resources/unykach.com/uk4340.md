## Overview

4U 19" rackmount server chassis (600mm depth) designed for high-end workstations, professional rendering, and compute-intensive tasks. Features 3x 120mm PWM fans included, USB-C support, support for large format GPUs including NVIDIA RTX 4090/5090, and liquid cooling up to 360mm. Includes expansion card retention system and 10-year warranty.
