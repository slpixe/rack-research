## Overview

4U 19" rackmount chassis with modern USB Type-C connectivity. Supports ATX, Micro-ATX, Mini-ITX, and Semi Extended ATX motherboards. Features front dust filter, adjustable expansion card holder, anti-intrusion security system, and front handles for easy rack management. Includes 1 ODD bay and ample storage options.
