## Overview

2U 19" rackmount chassis (600mm depth) for ATX and Micro-ATX motherboards. Features advanced cooling with 5 fans included (2x 80mm front, 3x 80mm PWM center), USB-C support, and can accommodate high-performance GPUs including RTX 4090. Supports liquid cooling radiators up to 360mm.
