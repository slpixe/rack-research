## Overview

4U 19" rackmount chassis with ATX, Micro-ATX, and Mini-ITX support. Features 480mm depth, front key lock security, dust filter, 3 fans included (1x 120mm rear, 2x 80mm side), and front handles for easy rack management. Graphics card support up to 270mm in length.
